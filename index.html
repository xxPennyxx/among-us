Among Us - Beginner's Guide

Overview

Among Us is a multiplayer game of survival and betrayal, developed by Innersloth in 2018. It features up to 15 players in a lobby, who are either crewmates or impostors.

Roles

Crewmates

The main objective of the Crewmates is to finish the tasks assigned to them. If all the crewmates finish their tasks, they win the game. They must also fix sabotaged equipment such as Comms, lights, and O2 in order to survive.

Impostors

The Impostors are the 'bad guys' of the game, who have to mingle with the crew while secretly sabotaging ship equipment and killing crewmates. Impostors can pass through vents to make their job easier. Since November 2021, impostors can also disguise themselves to other crewmates to confuse players. In July 2024, impostors gained the ability to take on the role of Phantoms, allowing them to vanish for short periods, making the game even harder.

Special Roles

Roles in Detail

  • Scientist: Can access vitals from anywhere but needs to recharge the battery by completing tasks.
  • Engineer: Can use vents but has a cooldown and can only stay inside for a limited time.
  • Shapeshifter: An Impostor who can morph into other crewmates or impostors. Time-limited transformation.
  • Guardian Angel: Dead crewmates can become Guardian Angels, protecting other crewmates from being killed.
  • Tracker: Can track the presence of crewmates. If the tracking freezes, the crewmate is dead.
  • Noisemaker: Emits noise after they die, helping others track the location of their death.
  • Phantom: An Impostor that can vanish for a limited time and cannot kill during this time but can be tracked by the Tracker.

Gameplay

How to Play

At the start of the game, players are randomly assigned the role of Crewmate or Impostor. Players must keep their role secret, except during meetings or reports. If you think someone is the Impostor, you can call an Emergency Meeting or report a dead body to discuss. The voting process follows, and the person with the most votes gets ejected. The game reveals whether or not they were an Impostor.

Tips & Tricks for Impostors

Impostor Strategy

  • Do not attempt self-reports immediately after a kill!
  • Time your kills wisely and always check your kill cooldown.
  • Avoid faking visual tasks like MedBay scan, asteroids, and trash!
  • Never fake being AFK anywhere!
  • Avoid killing or venting where cameras can see you.
  • Watch out for your shift and vent cooldowns!

Hide-and-Seek Mode

How Hide-and-Seek Works

In the Hide-and-Seek mode, the Impostor (the Seeker) is revealed to everyone. The Crewmates get 10 seconds to hide. After that, the Seeker has a limited amount of time to find and kill all the Crewmates. The Seeker starts with low vision, but during the final buffer time, they gain a speed boost and can track the Crewmates every 5 seconds. Crewmates must finish tasks to survive, and if they die, they can help track others still in the game.

If at least two Crewmates survive, they win the game!
